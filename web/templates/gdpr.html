General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) Agreement

Introduction

We are committed to safeguarding the privacy of our website visitors; in this policy, we explain how we will handle your personal data.

How we use your personal data

We may process data about your use of our website and services. The usage data may include your email address, geographical location, browser type and version, and other data regarding your interaction with our website. The source of the usage data is our analytics tracking system. Noticing that our app will use your address information to verify your location, but it will not save your address information in our database

Providing your personal data to others

We may disclose your personal data to our insurers and/or professional advisers as reasonably necessary for the purposes of obtaining insurance coverage, managing risks, obtaining professional advice, or the establishment, exercise, or defense of legal claims.

Your rights

You have the right to confirmation as to whether or not we process your personal data and, where we do, access to the personal data, together with certain additional information. You also have the rights to the rectification of your data, to erasure, to restrict processing, to object to processing, and to data portability.

About cookies

A cookie is a file containing an identifier sent by a web server to a web browser and stored by the browser. We use cookies for the following purposes: authentication, status and security.
