Game Instructions

  1. Navigate to the target point on the map.
  2. Click the "I've arrived! " button. The system will automatically check your location.
  3. If your location matches, the "Start Your adventure" button will be displayed.
  4. Click the "Start Your adventure" button to begin the challenge !
  5. Earn two points for every correct answer!
  6. Once you've answered all the questions, the next target point will appear. Continue your adventure!
